The Amelia yacht

Set sail in elegance aboard Amelia, our Beneteau Oceanis 51.1, available for bareboat or skippered charter from Athens. Designed for those who seek comfort, style, and performance, Amelia is the perfect yacht for unforgettable sailing holidays in Greece, exploring the Saronic Gulf, the Cyclades, or further into the Aegean Sea.

With her spacious and luminous interior, Amelia combines refined design with intelligent functionality. She comfortably accommodates up to 12 guests in 6 well-appointed cabins, offering generous living spaces for both relaxation and entertainment.

Enjoy a cool and pleasant onboard experience at all times with three independent air-conditioning units powered by a reliable generator—making life at sea as comfortable as on shore. Her modern sail plan and powerful hull ensure fast, stable, and enjoyable cruising in any wind conditions.

Beyond weekly charters, Peppersail also offers daily cruises, private events, and tailor-made experiences aboard Amelia—ideal for birthdays, anniversaries, corporate outings, or sunset escapes with family and friends.

Step aboard Amelia and discover the perfect blend of luxury, performance, and freedom—crafted for your next Greek island adventure.
